## Notice

**This HIP has been deprecated and moved to Zoo Labs Foundation.**

Decentralized Semantic Optimization (DSO) was originally developed as part of Hanzo's research but has been properly attributed to **Zoo Labs Foundation** as ZIP-001, reflecting the correct ownership and focus:

- **Hanzo AI Inc** provides the base infrastructure:
  - HIP-002 (ASO): Active Semantic Optimization with TF-GRPO and PoE
  - HIP-004 (HMM): Hamiltonian Market Maker for compute pricing
  - PoAI: Proof of AI consensus mechanism

- **Zoo Labs Foundation** extends with decentralization:
  - ZIP-001 (DSO): Byzantine-robust prior aggregation
  - Decentralized storage (IPFS/Arweave)
  - Multi-agent consensus mechanisms
